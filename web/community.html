Andela Learning Community

The goal of the ALC to provide an ever-growing population of aspiring technologists with the resources and environments they need to become world-class problem solvers.

The first community I joined in tech was ALC, I was a learner in ALC 2.0 in 2017, although I didn't finish the program, that was my first deep dive into anything software, I learned a lot during the program, and I was happy to be a part of it.

Facebook Developer Circle

In 2019 I was an LCA (learning community ambassador) in the Facebook Developer circle training with Andela, I was happy to have offered technical help to my learners either directly or indirectly and monitored their progress during the program on the Open Classroom platform, I wouldn't have done this without the support of my awesome Program Assistants (Emma Odia and Racheal Onoja).

Google Africa Developer Scholarship

This was the program that prompted the huge decision I made in June 2019. the program consisted of 30,000 Learners in 6 countries. 30,000 leaners were streamlined into 1000 Learners in a space of 5 months, #150DaysofAlc4. I am so excited to be a part of the 1000 learners that will be taking the google mobile web specialist exam in January 2020.

Unstack
